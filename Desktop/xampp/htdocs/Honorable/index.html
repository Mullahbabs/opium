Ongoing projects

Honorable Peter Recently embarked on a milestone project to pioneer the digitalization of the OBO constituency in line with the fast growing technological advancement of the world at large.

This project aims to create a working database for the entire constituency, arming each conctituent with a QRcode embeded identity card that upon scan verifies the identity of said constituent allowing for enhanced security, efficient outreach and effective governance within the constituency

This project aside its numerious advantages and importance happens to be the first of its kind in not just cross river but Nigeria as a whole and indeed the entire west africa.
